Frequently Asked Questions

Janitorial Program — Answered.

What does a janitorial program include?

−
Programs are customized per location and can include daily floor care, restroom sanitization, trash and recycling removal, surface wiping, window cleaning, equipment exterior degreasing, walk-in cooler maintenance, and scheduled deep cleaning rotations. We build the scope around your operating hours and specific needs.

How is pricing structured?

−
Contract pricing per location based on square footage, frequency, scope of work, and operating hours. We provide a custom quote after a free facility walkthrough. Multi-location operators receive consolidated pricing under one contract — one invoice for all locations.

Are your crews bilingual?

+
Yes. TruShine operates bilingual crews (English and Spanish) across all service areas. Clear communication with your kitchen staff and management regardless of language preference.

Can I add janitorial to my existing TruShine contract?

+
Yes — and most clients do. Adding janitorial consolidates another vendor, gives you one invoice, and ensures the same quality standards across all services. Your account manager handles everything.

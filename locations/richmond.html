Richmond Services

Every service available à la carte or bundled. All services include digital compliance documentation.

Hood Cleaning

NFPA 96 compliant exhaust hood, duct, and fan cleaning. Full documentation and compliance reporting included. Overnight service.

Fire Suppression

Brand-certified semi-annual inspection and repair. Ansul, Amerex, Badger, Pyrochem, Buckeye, Captive Aire.

Grease Trap Service

Pumping, cleaning, and compliant disposal. Prevents FOG violations and Virginia Department of Health (VDH) fines. Scheduled programs available by tank size.

UCO Management

Deep Cleaning

Full kitchen restoration — walls, floors, equipment, walk-ins. BOH and FOH. Health inspection ready documentation included.

Janitorial Program

Daily, weekly, and monthly cleaning programs. Bilingual crews. Contract pricing per location.

Drain Jetting

High-pressure hydro jetting for kitchen drains, grease lines, and sewer connections. Emergency and scheduled service available.

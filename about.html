The Origin

He Didn't Study Kitchens. He Worked In Them.

Carlos Tavarez started at the bottom. Dishwasher. 2004. Just like thousands of people who walk into Atlanta restaurants every year looking for a shot. He took that shot seriously.

Over 11 years, he worked his way through every station — prep, line, grill, sauté — absorbing the rhythm of a professional kitchen. The inspection anxiety before a health visit. The compliance scramble when the fire marshal showed up unannounced. The hood cleaning vendors who showed up late, did a partial job, and left no documentation. He saw every version of it.

He rose to Executive Sous Chef at Davio's Atlanta — one of the city's most demanding fine dining kitchens. At that level, you run the kitchen. You own compliance. You're the one who gets the call when something goes wrong.

"I built TruShine because I knew from the inside what restaurants needed and what most vendors weren't delivering. When I walk into a kitchen, I'm not a cleaning guy. I'm someone who worked the line, ran the service, and dealt with every compliance issue you're dealing with right now."

— Carlos Tavarez, Founder & CEO

In 2015, Carlos founded TruShine Services in Atlanta with a single principle: build the compliance company he wished had existed when he was running kitchens. Not a janitorial service that added hood cleaning. A dedicated kitchen compliance partner — built from the inside out.
